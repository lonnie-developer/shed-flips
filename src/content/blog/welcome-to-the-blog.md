If you've landed here, you probably already watched a video on the [Shed Flips YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/@shedflips) and thought, *"I want to come back to that haul list later — is it written down somewhere?"*

Now it is.

## What this blog is

Hi — we're Lonnie and Candice, and we buy stuff at garage sales, estate sales, auctions, and thrift stores, then sell it online (eBay, Mercari, Etsy, Poshmark — wherever it lands best). The YouTube channel is where we walk you through it on camera. This blog is the written companion: every post here ties back to a specific video, with the embed right at the top so you can:

- **Watch the haul** the way it was filmed, or
- **Skim the write-up** for the finds, the prices we paid, what sold, and what we'd do differently next time, or
- **Do both** — keep the video playing while you scan the list.

Posts will lean toward the *useful* end: actual numbers, actual platforms, actual mistakes. The kind of detail that's easier to read than rewind for.

## What it isn't

It's not a replacement for the channel — that's still where everything happens first, and the videos are the canonical record. Think of this as the place to come back to when you want to look something up: "what was that beer-tap-handle haul priced at?" or "which platform did that thing actually sell on?"

## What's coming

We'll work backward through the catalog over time, posting written companions for the videos that have the most going on — strategy talks, big hauls, sold-stats wrap-ups. New videos will get a post around when they drop.

If you're new, the easiest way to keep up is to [subscribe on YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@shedflips) — the blog's just a more permanent home for the same stories.

Come on in — welcome back to the shed.
