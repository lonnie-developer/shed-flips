Daily UPS pickup is the hard stop. We aim to get at least one UPS box per day so the driver keeps coming back. Today's 10 orders need to be palletized and ready for the afternoon pull. Two-hour window, roughly.

Box selection and carrier strategy changes daily based on weight and destination. Ground Advantage is aggressive right now on smaller boxes, but oversized shipments still favor UPS. That math dictates what goes to which carrier — pickup reliability depends on it.
