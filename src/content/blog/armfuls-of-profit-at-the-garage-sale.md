Quick one. We hit a Saturday yard sale where the seller looked up and said, *"You've been buying a bunch of HBA lately, huh?"* — meaning health-and-beauty-aids, the cosmetics-and-skincare reseller category. The fact that we're now recognizable by what we *buy* at her sale tells you most of what you need to know about how often we're showing up.

Pulled an armful: a few pairs of Vans (interesting colors — pre-owned at $1 a pair), a stack of khakis, and the seller throwing extras in once she saw the pile growing. *"That's the good thing about eBay sellers, we do spend money."* Yes ma'am. We'll be back next weekend.
