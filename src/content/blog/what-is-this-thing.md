Candice picked up a used wooden candle off a yard-sale table and was already sold. *"It's not used very much."* Then she asked the seller, *"Is it primitive?"*

The seller said it was probably from the eighties or nineties.

I had to step in. *"That's not primitive, Candace. We had these when I was a kid."* Primitive is a category. Eighties wood is just... wood.

She kept saying primitive. I kept saying not primitive. And then I floated my actual best guess for what the thing was: *"You sure it's not a reflex tester?"*

She bought it anyway. *"If we can't sell it, I'll drink coffee out of it."*
