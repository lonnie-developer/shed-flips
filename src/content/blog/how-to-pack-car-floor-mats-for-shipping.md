Eighteen orders across the day. **[<strong>Car floor mats</strong>](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ddk7amruvOQ&t=0s)** need padding and room to spread out flat. Roll them, bag them if they're fragrant or dirty, and pick a box tall enough for the diameter. Heavy and bulky — Ground Advantage box choice matters here.

**[<strong>$190 photo sticker printer</strong>](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ddk7amruvOQ&t=0s)** going out. Sealed electronics pack straightforward. **Urban Decay eyeliners** (we keep needing to source a pallet of these — they move consistently). Pop Mart blind boxes (never can tell what's inside, but we verify figure vs. box before shipping). Fostoria glass, which moves through either eBay or local buyers depending on pattern and color.

Mix-of-everything pack day. Box selection on weight class usually determines carrier — Ground Advantage wins below 5 lb on most regional routes, UPS takes over above that depending on destination zone.
