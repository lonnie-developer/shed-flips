# Sourcing notes — Things got kinda crazy in the shed today

Source video: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dxRRm765UsA (2026-04-23)

## What this video actually is

Despite the chaotic title, this is a **shipping-day video plus a listing
session**. Two-act structure:

- **Act 1 (00:00 – ~16:00):** pulling and packing every order across eBay and
  Mercari, item by item. 21 individual eBay sales pulled, plus one Mercari
  bundle.
- **Act 2 (~17:00 – 23:00):** Candice dumps two trash-bag-sized "$10 filler
  bag" lots of yarn/ribbon/buttons in the middle of the floor, they sort and
  lot it up, and Lonnie tackles his paintball-marker lot and a skateboard.

## Inside-jokes and channel beats from this episode

- **"Express to Guam"** — Lonnie's stand-in for the high end of the eBay
  shipping-cost range. Re-usable shorthand for "ignore the inflated upper
  bound when you're estimating."
- **"Solid gold." "No, it's not."** — Lonnie tests whether Candice is
  listening when he describes the Elks pin. She catches it.
- **"Per purveyor of fine driving shoes"** — Lonnie joke when shipping the
  Steve Madden Kronos loafers. Continues the running Rev Resale joke
  (size 9.5 D matches Rev's shoe size, allegedly).
- **"Buy it because the price is right. List it like in the most expedient
  way possible."** — Lonnie philosophy line. Distilled rule: don't over-
  invest research time on cheap-buy speculative inventory; price-in the
  uncertainty by listing fast.
- **"We should have waited for the full price."** — said about the $60
  Mercari boot-cover bundle (4 styles plus 1 dup). They could've gotten more
  parting them out but accepted the bundle. Mirrors prior "we left a little
  money on the table, but we do that often" phrasing.
- **"Fettucini"** — Lonnie's random one-word reaction to Candice's pile of
  yarn. No deeper meaning.
- **The "I don't want to get into that too much"** — Lonnie referring to the
  Kindra Daily V Lotion (intimate-skin moisturizer). Continuing the
  understated dry-humor pattern with brand awkwardness (cf. Smashbox primer
  in the guy drawer).
